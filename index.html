Global Resources

Artisanal Ingredients from Around the World

Indulge in the rich heritage of Morocco and the elegance of France with our artisanal soaps, crafted with the finest ingredients sourced from around the world. Our Moroccan Akarfassi Patel powder and Moroccan indigo powder are carefully harvested from the Atlas Mountains, renowned for their potent properties.

We blend these treasures with luxurious mango butter from Morocco and the soothing essence of lavender from France, complemented by the delicate charm of Turkish herbal tea flowers. Each soap is a masterpiece, combining global inspiration with natural excellence to nourish and pamper your skin.

Handcrafted Excellence
