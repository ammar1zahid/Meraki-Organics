Our Beginnings

The Meraki Journey

Meraki Organics was born from a passion for natural beauty and a commitment to sustainability. Our founder, Rodaba Ejaz, began creating homemade skincare remedies using traditional family recipes passed down through generations.

What started as a small kitchen experiment has now grown into a beloved brand that combines ancient Ayurvedic knowledge with modern cosmetic science. The name "Meraki" comes from the Greek word meaning "to do something with soul, creativity, and love" â€“ a philosophy that guides everything we create.

Today, we continue to handcraft our products in small batches, ensuring quality and freshness while staying true to our roots. Every formula is developed with intention, thoroughly tested, and made with carefully sourced ingredients that honor both nature and tradition.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Our core values guide every decision we make and product we create

Natural Purity

We believe in the power of nature's purest ingredients. Our formulations harness the potency of organic botanicals, essential oils, and plant extracts without compromising on efficacy.

Sustainability

Our commitment to the planet extends beyond ingredients to our packaging, operations, and business practices. We continuously strive to minimize our environmental footprint.

Ethical Production

We ensure fair trade practices with our ingredient suppliers and maintain cruelty-free certifications across our entire product range.

Innovation

While we honor traditional wisdom, we continuously research and develop new formulations that merge ancient knowledge with scientific advancement.

Community

We believe in building meaningful relationships with our customers, suppliers, and the communities where we source our ingredients.

Transparency

We believe in clear communication about our ingredients, processes, and business practices. No hidden ingredients, no misleading claims.
