Frequent Questions

Common Inquiries

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our products and services

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates vary based on location and package size. Delivery typically takes 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Products must be unused, in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?

Most of our products are formulated for sensitive skin. However, we always recommend doing a patch test first. Check individual product descriptions for specific information.

Do you offer wholesale options?

Yes, we offer wholesale options for retailers and spas. Please contact us at wholesale@merakiorganics.com for pricing details and minimum order requirements.
